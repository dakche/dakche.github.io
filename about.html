About Me

Having come from a non-engineering background, I've been teaching myself engineering concepts
and skills using the internet and books and applying my skills through independent projects
and school projects. I am skilled in mechanical CAD and FEA analysis, electrical circuit design
and simulation, and embedded microcontroller programming to complete most mid-sized projects
by myself, but I enjoy working with and learning from other people more knowledgable than me.
My interest is lies in power electronics including DC-DC converters, motor control,
battery charging, solar conversion, grid storage, etc.
